About ACM SIGBED

ACM SIGBED (Special Interest Group on Embedded Systems) is a professional community dedicated to embedded and cyber-physical systems. SIGBED provides a vibrant platform for researchers, practitioners, and students to explore the fascinating world of embedded systems, real-time computing, and IoT technologies. The group organizes conferences, workshops, and publications that advance the state-of-the-art in embedded systems design, analysis, and applications. SIGBED's mission is to foster collaboration, knowledge sharing, and innovation in the embedded systems domain.

About KARE ACM SIGBED Student Chapter

The KARE ACM SIGBED Student Chapter is an official student chapter of ACM SIGBED, established at Kalasalingam Academy of Research and Education. Our chapter emphasizes technical innovation, mentorship, and real-world applications aligned with the global SIGBED mission. We provide students with opportunities to engage in hands-on learning, research projects, and professional development activities.

Our core focus: hands-on tutorials, project showcases, expert lectures, and collaborative engagement in embedded and real-time systems. Through workshops, technical sessions, hackathons, and competitions, we aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements, preparing our members for successful careers in embedded systems and related fields.
